Welcome to the PENTOMINO study!

In this study from the University of Potsdam you will be instructed by an artificial agent to pick pentomino objects.
It is important that you can clearly hear the spoken instructions.

You can only do this study on a computer, but not on a mobile device (no tablet or phone).

It is also important that you try to make as few mistakes as possible in the task. You will be paid more per correct answer.

If any problems occur with the interface, please let the experimenter know before you complete the task.

This task will last 10 minutes.


If you have any questions, do not hesitate to contact Dimos Kontogiorgos at kontogiorgos@uni-potsdam.de.
